Welcome to Knowledge Views

Capture pages, highlight the web, and search your notes — all saved as plain files in your Obsidian vault, on this computer. There's no account, and nothing ever leaves your machine.

  1. 1

    Connect your vault

    Pair once with Obsidian's Browser bridge — in Obsidian, open Settings → Browser bridge, turn it on, generate a pairing code, and paste it in the companion's settings. This is the only setup step.

    Checking your vault…
  2. 2

    Turn on web highlighting

    Select text on any page and a toolbar appears — highlight in eight colours, add a note, copy, or search it. Highlights are saved to your vault and painted again when you revisit. Needs permission to read the pages you highlight.

  3. 3

    The tools you'll reach for

    • Select text → a toolbar to highlight, copy, search the selection, or drop a sticky note.
    • The edge minimap — a coloured dot for every highlight down the page edge; click to jump.
    • Sticky notes you can drag, resize, and write markdown in, pinned to the page.
    • Number keys 1–8 highlight the selection fast; brush mode highlights everything you select.
    • Search your whole vault — notes, rows, highlights, and PDFs — from the toolbar or the popup.

You can reopen this guide any time from Settings.
