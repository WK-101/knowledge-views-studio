How it opens

The sidebar is the fuller surface: it stays open while you browse and adds your views, so you can work through a reading queue, edit rows and search without switching applications. Open it from your browser's sidebar control — in Firefox, View → Sidebar → Knowledge Views.

Mark search results

Off by default. Turning it on asks for access to search sites — Google, Scholar, DuckDuckGo, Bing, Brave, arXiv, PubMed and Semantic Scholar — in one go, rather than one at a time. Only the addresses of results are checked, and only against your own vault; nothing about what you have is sent back to the page beyond which links it recognised.
